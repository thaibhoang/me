# Project Scope

## Product Vision
Xay dung mot website ca nhan theo huong "personal brand platform" de:
- Chia se kien thuc va trai nghiem lam viec.
- Trung bay du an da thuc hien voi bang chung ro rang.
- Chuyen doi nguoi xem thanh co hoi hop tac/tuyen dung.

## Target Audience
- Recruiter, hiring manager, tech lead.
- Dong nghiep trong cong dong cong nghe.
- Khach hang tiem nang can freelance/contract.

## Core Goals
- Viet bai deu dan, de tim thay tren Google.
- Portfolio ro rang, de chung minh nang luc va ket qua.
- Co diem chot chuyen doi: contact, booking, hoac "Hire me".

## Required Pages

### Home (Landing)
- Headline ngan gon va ro proposition.
- Section "Featured Projects".
- Section "Latest Posts".
- CTA ro rang: "Hire Me", "View Portfolio", "Contact".

### Blog
- Danh sach bai viet theo list/grid.
- Filter theo category/tag.
- Sap xep theo ngay xuat ban moi nhat.

### Portfolio
- Danh sach du an.
- Moi du an co:
  - Hinh anh/video demo.
  - Bai toan va ket qua.
  - Cong nghe su dung.
  - Link GitHub va Live Demo (neu co).

### About
- Hanh trinh nghe nghiep, gia tri, triet ly lam viec.
- Ky nang chinh va linh vuc manh.
- CV online + nut tai PDF.

### Contact
- Form lien he toi gian.
- Danh sach kenh: LinkedIn, GitHub, Email.

## Cross-page Feature: Tech Stack Badge
- Ap dung cho ca bai viet va du an.
- Click vao badge (vd: Rails, AWS, React) de loc toan bo noi dung lien quan.
- Muc tieu: nguoi xem thay nhanh "nang luc theo cong nghe".

## Primary CTAs
- Hire Me
- View Projects
- Read Blog
- Contact

## KPI Tracking
- `contact_submit_rate`: Ty le gui form lien he.
- `project_click_through`: Ty le click vao project detail/live demo.
- `resume_download_rate`: Ty le tai CV.
- `post_read_depth`: Ti le doc sau bai viet (scroll depth/time on page).

## Non-goals (Phase dau)
- Khong xay social network noi bo.
- Khong lam CMS da role phuc tap.
- Khong toi uu da ngon ngu ngay tu dau (uu tien 1 ngon ngu).
